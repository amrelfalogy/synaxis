Who We Are

The roots of Synaxis Company and its main headquarters go back to the Emirate of Abu Dhabi after the success of engineer Mohamed Kamal in the Arab Gulf countries through project management and general contracting, which led to gaining experience through the implementation of approximately 60 projects inside and outside Egypt for a period exceeding 20 years. Here, plans were made and the foundation stone was laid to establish the Synaxis entity inside Egypt in order to complete the journey of success within Egypt and provide a high level of services.

Our Vision

Synaxis Company aspires to be the first and even the largest business leader in the region and at the level of the Middle East, through planned businesses and partnerships with both the private and government sectors. It also aspires to raise the employment rate and raise the level of efficiency, experience and work standards, in addition to contributing with the government and the private sector. And businessmen to move forward with our investments in all sectors and achieve profits together.
